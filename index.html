DON'T BREAK THE ICE !!

visibility_off visibility
    HOW TO PLAY
  • Take turns to tap out the ice blocks!
  • Once tapped, the blocks cannot be tapped on again!
  • Do not tap on Peng the Penguin!
  • To win, force your opponent to drop Peng!
    HOW TO DROP?
  • Click on the tile to make it fall!
  • If a tile does not have adjacent tiles, it will fall too!
    Example: If Tile 1 has already fallen and you pick tile 2, tile 3 and 4 will fall
  • Peng falls when there are no more tiles on opposite sides.
    These are the minimum support required, on either sides!
